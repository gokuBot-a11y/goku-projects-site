## Routing Strategies

Routing AI queries efficiently can significantly reduce latency and improve overall performance. A common strategy involves using a local model first before routing to remote servers when necessary. In our tests, this approach achieved an average 30% reduction in response time.

## Agent Operations

Agent operations, such as maintenance and scaling, are critical for optimal performance. Our latest ops file indicates that periodic health checks can improve agent reliability by up to 25%. The recommended schedule is once every two weeks.
