## AI Usage Efficiency
AI systems are becoming increasingly sophisticated, but their efficient deployment remains a challenge. Recent studies suggest that optimizing model performance can reduce energy consumption by up to 70% [1]. For instance, a deep learning model running on an optimized hardware setup can achieve this reduction without compromising accuracy or speed.

## Model Pricing
Pricing AI models is not just about cost; it's also about sustainability. According to industry benchmarks, a large language model (LLM) with 10 billion parameters might cost around $250 per month for continuous training [2]. This includes cloud infrastructure costs and operational expenses.

## Routing Mechanisms
Routing AI traffic efficiently is crucial for both performance and economic reasons. Intelligent routing can reduce latency by up to 40% in a multi-cloud environment, making it more feasible to distribute workloads across different regions [3]. For example, using edge computing can significantly speed up local requests while offloading heavy computations to cloud servers.
